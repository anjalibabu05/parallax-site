"Harry Potter" is a fantasy book and film series created by J.K. Rowling, centered around a young wizard named Harry Potter and his journey through Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Along with his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, Harry battles the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who seeks immortality and domination over the wizarding and non-wizarding worlds.
